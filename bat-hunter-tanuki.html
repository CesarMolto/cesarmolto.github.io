BAT HUNTER TANUKI: THE REVENGE

Bat Hunter Tanuki is a 2D action roguelike game inspired by Japanese folklore and originally developed in one week for the 2 Buttons Jam 2018.

To recover your precious leaves you must kill all the enemies and get to the end of the randomly generated maze where the Midnight Lord awaits.

Platform: Browser
Project length: 1 week


PLAY IN BROWSER

OVERVIEW

Bat Hunter Tanuki was originally created in one week for the 2 Buttons Jam 2018 and was a great chance to discover my limits at the time of developing a whole game in a short period of time.

The game is mainly inspired by rogue like games I really enjoyed such as The Binding of Isaac, Enter the Gungeon or Moonlighter.

Developing this game was super challenging, satisfactory, and a very good opportunity to research and implement roguelike concepts that I love such as procedurally generated environments and permanent death.

WHAT I DID

• Programmed a procedural maze generation algorithm.

• Created a final boss with different special attacks.

• Explored different concepts to make the game playable with just two buttons.

• Created a dialogue system to tell the story behind the game.

• Created all the art assets.

• Created all the sound effects (but the music).

• Programmed the whole game.

• Designed the UI and gameplay being aware of the 2 buttons limitation.
