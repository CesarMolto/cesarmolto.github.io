NOBODY

Nobody is a platformer and puzzle game, developed for the Games Fleadh 2019.

In order to solve the puzzles of the game you will need to kill the enemies of every level and use the body change mechanic to get into places that otherwise you would not be able to reach.

Platform: Browser
Team size: 1 (myself)
Project length: 3 weeks


PLAY IN BROWSER

OVERVIEW

When I created Nobody I did it because I wanted to explore new ideas and mechanics which I thought could be interesting to see in a platformer/puzzle game.

I have always loved puzzle games like Braid, Limbo, Portal, etc... And with Nobody I had the chance to my first game of this genre and improve my level desing skills, creating challenging puzzles that could give great satisfaction to the players when solving them.

The goal I had when decided to create Nobody was to explore a genre of games that I never did before, and try to do something different that could be interesting for the players.

WHAT I DID

- Created all the art assets.

- Created all the sound effects (but the music).

- Designed the levels..

- Programmed the mechanics.

- Designed the UI and gameplay.
