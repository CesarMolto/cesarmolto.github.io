ABOUT ME

SOCIAL MEDIA

Hello there!

My name is Cesar, and I'm a passionate Computer Graphics R&D Engineer with expertise in developing and integrating advanced rendering features in custom and commercial game engines (UE4, UE5, Unity) using DirectX12, Vulkan, and OpenGL/WebGL. I specialize in real-time rendering for both rasterization and ray tracing, shader programming, and performance optimization across PC, XR, and mobile platforms. Currently, I'm also pursuing a PhD at the Rey Juan Carlos University, researching physically accurate fabric rendering.

Below you'll see some of the projects I have worked on. If you find my work interesting, please do not hesitate to contact me!


RESUME

PROFESSIONAL

FINAL FANTASY VII: REBIRTH - PC VERSION

Supported the development of the Final Fantasy VII Rebirth PC port from PS5 by optimizing mesh streaming in a Nanite-inspired custom system in UE4 for rendering massive environments.

Performance improvements of 30–50% were achieved by adapting the streaming system to account for platform-specific memory differences and mesh handling, ensuring efficient runtime performance and load times.

OFFICIAL SITE

TITAN QUEST: LEGENDARY EDITION

Contributed to the mobile adaptation of Titan Quest: Legendary Edition, implementing rendering features for UI elements and controller-specific graphics and animations.

Integrated full gamepad support, extended gameplay logic, and optimized both memory usage and rendering performance to ensure a smooth experience across mobile devices.

RESEARCH

UNREAL HOME

Unreal Home is a simulation platform designed to carry out the generation of photorealistic synthetic datasets of human daily basis interactions in virtual environments.

It was developed in collaboration with the 3D Perception Lab (3DPL) group from the Department of Information Technology and Computing (DTIC) of the University of Alicante.
