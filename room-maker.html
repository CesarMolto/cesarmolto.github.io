ROOM MAKER

Room maker is a Virtual Reality room editor where you can recreate house rooms, adding different furniture and elements to them.

Thanks to the VR technology, you can get into the different rooms you created, and see how they look like from a more realistic first person point of view.

Platform: PC (WINDOWS)
Team size: 1 (myself)
Project length: 3 months


DOWNLOAD (WINDOWS)

OVERVIEW

I have been always really fond of the Virtual Reality technology and Room Maker was my chance to approach and explore the VR world.

I decided to develop Room Maker one year ago to solve a problem of mine. My mother wanted to get a new bed for my really small room and I had to choose between different bed sizes. The matter was that I wanted to have the biggest bed I could get but at the same time I wanted to have as much free space in my room as possible.

However, I really could not know how much free space I would have in my small room with each bed size. Then, at that moment, I thought about developing a piece of software that allows people to know how the furniture they want to buy for their houses will look before buying them thanks to the VR technology.

When I finished Room Maker, it worked better than I expected and I got the perfect bed for my room.

This is one of the projects I am most proud of because it was a really ambitious project and I finished it thanks to my hard work and dedication during 3 months.

WHAT I DID
