ROOM MAKER

Room Maker is a Virtual Reality scenario editor developed using Unity 3D and Oculus hardware.

Using Room Maker users can create their own indoor scenarios with different lighting and furniture and then explore them using VR technology.

Platform: PC (WINDOWS)
Project length: 3 months


DOWNLOAD (WINDOWS)

OVERVIEW

I have always been really interested in Virtual Reality, and Room Maker allowed me to research and explore this incredible technology.

I decided to develop Room Maker one year ago to solve a problem of mine. I wanted to get a new bed for my room, and I had to choose between different bed sizes. The matter was that I wanted to have the biggest bed I could get, but at the same time, I wanted to have as much free space in my room as possible. However, I could not know how much free space I would have in my room with each bed size. At that moment, the idea of developing a piece of software that allows people to know how different furniture would look in their houses thanks to the VR technology came to my mind.

In the end, Room Maker worked better than I expected, and I was able to find the perfect solution for my problem.

WHAT I DID

• Designed and programmed the whole application.

• Explored and learned about using VR technology in Unity 3D.

• Modeled some of the 3D and created all the 2D assets.

• Designed the User Interface and the User Interaction.

• Programmed a gizmo shadeer for moving the objects in the scenario.
