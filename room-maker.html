ROOM MAKER

Room Maker is a Virtual Reality scenario editor developed using Unity 3D and Oculus hardware.

Using Room Maker users can create their own indoor scenarios with different lighting and furniture, and explore them using VR technology.

Platform: Windows


DOWNLOAD (WINDOWS)

OVERVIEW

I have always been really interested in Virtual Reality and Room Maker allowed me to research and explore deeper this incredible technology.

Room Maker was created to solve a problem I had when I was in the process of getting new furniture for my house. I wanted to have the biggest bed I could fit in my room, but at the same time have as much free space left as possible. At that moment, the idea of developing a VR software application that would allow people see how furniture would look in their rooms before buying it came to my mind, and that is how Room Maker was born.

In the end, Room Maker worked better than I expected and I was able to find the perfect solution to my problem.

WHAT I DID

• Lead a 4 people team into developing a functional version of the software in 3 months.

• Created a gizmo shader for moving the objects through the scenario.

• Designed and programmed the most critical parts of the application.

• Explored and learned how to develop VR software in Unity 3D.

• Designed the User Interface and the User Interaction process.

• Created some of the 3D and 2D assets.
